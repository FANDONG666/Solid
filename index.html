    Shanghai Solid Fittings Co., Ltd, specializing in manufacturing all kinds of hydraulic connector: hose ferrule, hose fittings, flange, quick couplings and hydraulic adapter etc. Our products are strictly followed as the standard of American SAE, British BS, German DIN, Australian AS, and Chinese GB.

    Generally speaking, we are medium size factory in China in this field, professional, efficient with quick respond. We could offer acceptable quality and delivery, but better price and service. Our factory covers 3000 square meters, located in Hemudu industrial zone in Yuyao City, only 1 hour to Ningbo, and two hours to Shanghai.

    Started in 2002, we have been in this field over 15 years. We have 85 sets of manufacturing machines: 32 sets of CNC lathe, cutting machine, special cold holder machine etc, and 105 skilled workers and 10 experienced technician to carry out the quality control according to ISO 9001, which ensure the product in good quality.

    Our products are shiped to around the world, we are working with distributors in UK,Russia and Spain in Europe, US and Canada in North America, Peru and Brazil in South America, Israel and Saudi in Mid east etc. we are seeking partners for long term cooperation in this field, we expect to expand more business in the region for mutual benefit.
